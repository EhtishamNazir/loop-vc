PAY YOUR LOAN ON TIME

Responsible financial behaviour includes the way you approach your existing credit.
Always make your payments on time and try to aim for the same day each month.
To avoid missing payments or paying late, both of which have a negative impact on your credit score,
consider making your payments via debit order.
